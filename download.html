Sobre o jogo

Spotlight on Lamborghini in the Raging Bull season, with special attention to the Countach for its 50th Anniversary. It's also the b-day of Asphalt 9, so let's roll!

The fuel to get your pulse racing. Boost, drift and knock down your rivals in jacked-up real-world locations. Collect over 150 hypercars, and race with the most prestigious manufacturers, such as Ferrari, Porsche or Lamborghini. Get ready for greatness in this multi-award-winning arcade racing game! Race with the most amazing speed machines handpicked among world-renown manufacturers. Upgrade their stats, pick the color of their brake calipers or add carbon parts and more, to fully customize your rides.
